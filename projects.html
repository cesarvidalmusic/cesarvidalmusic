Visages
Duo · Saxophone & Live Electronics · 2024–

Europe-based duo of saxophone and live electronics formed by Chilean musicians César Vidal and Jorge Care. Their work is rooted in improvisation and spontaneous interaction, exploring the dialogue between acoustic and electronic sound to construct evolving sonic narratives. Through real-time performance, the duo transforms texture, rhythm, and space into expressive materials that blur the boundaries between composition and improvisation.

Currently developing Ghosts Evade Sin, a new recording project expanding their language toward a more structured relationship between improvisation, technology, and form — seven original pieces organized as a temporal palindrome.

Quintet · Contemporary Jazz · Netherlands

Multicultural contemporary jazz quintet based in the Netherlands, bringing together musicians from Chile, Italy, Portugal, and the Basque Country. Alto saxophone, piano, double bass, drums and electronics — exploring the intersection between acoustic and electronic sound worlds through composition and improvisation.

Their repertoire spans from simple, repetitive melodic patterns to intricate structures inspired by Olivier Messiaen and Iannis Xenakis. In 2025 the band opened for Cécile McLorin Salvant at Getxo Jazz Festival and was awarded second place in the festival's ensemble competition.

NUBES
Duo · With Nicolás Vera · 2022–

Duo project with renowned Chilean guitarist Nicolás Vera. Born from remote collaboration that began in 2022 when César moved to Amsterdam, the project resulted in the album NUBES (2025) — ten tracks exploring airy, fluid sonic landscapes. A second duo album is in preparation.
