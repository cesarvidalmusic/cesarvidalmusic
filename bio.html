Bio

César Vidal is a Chilean saxophonist, composer, and improviser. His practice explores the intersections between acoustic sound and digital processes, placing improvisation at the center of composition and performance.

He holds a Bachelor's in Composition from the University of Chile and a Master's in Jazz Saxophone from the Conservatorium van Amsterdam, where he studied with prominent Dutch musicians.

His career spans collaborations with key figures from different generations of the Chilean scene and performances across Europe and Chile, with highlights at BIMHUIS (Amsterdam), Getxo Jazz, and JazzBer (Basque Country).

He has appeared on acclaimed Chilean recordings and his recent projects include NUBES (with Nicolás Vera), Flor de Loto (with Joaquín Fuentes), and the debut EP of Des Visages. In 2025, with his ensemble César Vidal & Artikulation Band, he was awarded second place at the Getxo Jazz Bands Competition.

His artistic vision emphasizes improvisation as a critical tool for expanding the relationship between structure and freedom in contemporary and new sound practices.
